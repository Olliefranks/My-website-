What have i discovered..

During week 3, one new discovery that stood out to me was the importance of CSS specificity in styling web elements. I learned that CSS rules with higher specificity override those with lower specificity, leading to unexpected styling outcomes if not managed properly. This discovery prompted me to delve deeper into CSS specificity and flex-box structured approaches to styling, ultimately improving the consistency and efficiency of my frontend design process.
